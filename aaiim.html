Art And Imagination In Motion

Discover how art and science come together in Art and Imagination in Motion, an interactive experience that turns creativity into action! This activity invites you to experiment with movement, patterns, and physics to create stunning works of art.

Perfect for curious kids and imaginative families, this activity blends science, art, and fun, showing how creativity is powered by motion. Come and let your imagination run wild!

Activities:

  • Use pendulums and spinning platforms to paint mesmerizing patterns.
  • Explore the beauty of light and color with interactive digital art stations.
  • Build your own moving sculptures and watch your creations come alive.
  • Learn how motion and forces influence art in surprising and exciting ways.
  • Experiment with tools like paint spinners, wave makers, and light projectors to create unique designs.
  • Take home your masterpiece as a memory of your creative adventure.
