Dinosaurs & Ancient Life

Journey to a world where dinosaurs roamed and ancient life thrived! This immersive exhibit lets kids and families become paleontologists as they uncover the secrets of Earth’s prehistoric past.

Dinosaurs are always a favorite—get ready to roar with excitement!

Activities:

  • Marvel at life-sized dinosaur models and learn fun facts about their lives.
  • Dig into our fossil excavation zone and discover hidden treasures.
  • Step into a prehistoric VR simulation to experience the world of dinosaurs up close.
  • Hands-on activities that let you touch and feel replicas of ancient fossils.
  • Interactive timelines showcasing the evolution of life on Earth.
  • Animatronic dinosaurs that bring these ancient creatures to life.
