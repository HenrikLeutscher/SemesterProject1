Explore our Library

Step into a cozy space filled with knowledge and wonder! Our library is a treasure trove of science books, e-resources, and interactive media designed to spark curiosity in kids and their families.

Whether you're looking to expand your knowledge or simply enjoy a quiet moment, our library is the perfect place to fuel your love for science.

Activities:

  • Discover fascinating science facts in books tailored for young explorers.
  • Dive into augmented reality (AR) books that bring topics like space, dinosaurs, and biology to life.
  • Join science storytime sessions or tackle fun challenges from our science activity kits.
  • A relaxing space for families to learn and explore together.
  • Themed science corners like “Space Explorers” and “Dinosaur Detectives.”
  • Digital tablets loaded with interactive science apps and games.
