The Wonders of Space 

Prepare for an adventure that’s out of this world! The Wonders of Space takes you on an unforgettable journey through the stars, planets, and galaxies beyond.

The sky’s not the limit—discover the infinite possibilities of space!

Activities:

  • Explore a digital planetarium and travel through the solar system.
  • Build and launch your own mini rockets in a simulation lab.
  • Experience a VR spacewalk, where you’ll float among the stars like an astronaut.
  • Hands-on activities like creating constellations or designing your own planet.
  • Displays featuring real meteorites and the latest discoveries in space exploration.
  • An immersive space station area where kids can role-play as astronauts.
